## 🧠 Summary
A Rune Priest of the Space Wolves, Aun Helwintr played a key role in helping [[Kasper Hawser]] explore his psychically suppressed memories. Helwintr explained to [[Kasper Hawser|Hawser]] that his mind had been manipulated, causing memories to blur — the first clear indication that [[Kasper Hawser|Hawser]] was not with the Wolves by his own uninfluenced will. He fought in the assault on Prospero and was present during the confrontation with the Chaos entity that had engineered the conflict.

## 🔗 Connections
- Worked closely with [[Kasper Hawser]] to unravel the mystery of his past.
- Served under [[Leman Russ]] and alongside fellow Rune Priests [[Ohthere Wyrdmake]] and [[Ulvurul Heoroth]].
