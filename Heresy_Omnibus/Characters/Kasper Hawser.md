## 🧠 Summary
Kasper Ansbach Hawser was a Terran scholar and conservator who became the *skjald* — the saga-keeper — of Tra (Third) Company of the Space Wolves. Though he believed he came to Fenris through academic curiosity, the Wolves long suspected he had been psychically manipulated into joining them. Hawser's augmented body and strange connection to the Wolves' languages hinted at deeper interference in his life.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Prospero Burns|Prospero Burns]]
- Reconstructed after his craft was shot down over Fenris, Hawser was kept in induced stasis for nineteen Fenrisian years before awakening to serve as Tra Company's chronicler.
- At the Council of Nikaea, the Wolves revealed he was a deep-cover agent implanted by the Thousand Sons — a fact confirmed by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Amon (Thousand Sons)]].
- Present during the Burning of Prospero, where he uncovered the Chaos entity that had been manipulating both the Wolves and the Thousand Sons.
- Voluntarily entered indefinite stasis afterwards to prevent himself from ever being used as a weapon again.

## 🔗 Connections
- Trusted companion of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Bjorn]] ("Bear"), whose true name he was the last to learn.
- Chronicled the deeds of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Amlodhi Skarssen Skarssensson]]'s Tra Company.
- Interrogated by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Leman Russ]] at the Council of Nikaea and again after Prospero.
