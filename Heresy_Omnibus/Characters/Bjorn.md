## 🧠 Summary
Known throughout the *[[Prospero Burns]]* narrative simply as "Bear," Bjorn was a gruff and immensely powerful Space Wolf of Tra Company. He rescued [[Kasper Hawser]] from the Ascomanni ambush on Fenris and lost his arm to a Chaos entity during the Burning of Prospero — a loss that [[Kasper Hawser|Hawser]] prevented from becoming fatal by severing the burning limb. Bjorn's true name was a closely guarded secret among the Wolves; [[Kasper Hawser|Hawser]] was one of the last to learn it. He would survive the Heresy to become one of the greatest Dreadnoughts in the history of the Space Wolves.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Prospero Burns]]
- Rescued [[Kasper Hawser]] from the Ascomanni on Fenris, beginning their bond.
- Fought in the assault on Prospero alongside Tra Company.
- Faced and helped defeat the Chaos entity that had manipulated the conflict between the Wolves and the Thousand Sons, losing his arm in the process.

## 🔗 Connections
- Trusted companion and protector of [[Kasper Hawser]].
- Served under [[Amlodhi Skarssen Skarssensson]] in Tra Company.
- His long survival into the 41st Millennium as a Dreadnought makes him one of the oldest living Space Marines.
