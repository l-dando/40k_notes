## 🧠 Summary
Sanakht was renowned as one of the finest swordsmen among the Thousand Sons, a Legion known for its intellectual rigour and psychic mastery. His reputation as a blademaster made him a worthy adversary for [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius]] in the short story *[[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius|Lucius]] the Eternal Blademaster*, where both warriors — each legendary in their own right and now bearing the favour of Chaos — sought to determine which carried the greater martial gift.
