## 🧠 Summary
Sanakht was renowned as one of the finest swordsmen among the Thousand Sons, a Legion known for its intellectual rigour and psychic mastery. His reputation as a blademaster made him a worthy adversary for [[Lucius]] in the short story *[[Lucius]] the Eternal Blademaster*, where both warriors — each legendary in their own right and now bearing the favour of Chaos — sought to determine which carried the greater martial gift.

## 🔗 Connections
- Opponent and rival of [[Lucius]] during the events following the [[Emperor]]'s Children's departure from Iydris.
- Associated with [[Magnus the Red]]'s Thousand Sons.
