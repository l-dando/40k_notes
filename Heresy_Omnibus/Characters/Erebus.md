## 🧠 Summary
A manipulative and calculating Chaplain of the Word Bearers, travelling with the 63rd Expedition Fleet under diplomatic pretence.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Horus Rising|Horus Rising]]
- Secretly steals the Interex’s [[Heresy_Omnibus/Items/Anathame|anathame]] during negotiations.
- Sets in motion events that will have galaxy‑shaking consequences.
- Present during the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan III|Isstvan III]] betrayal as part of Horus’s inner circle.
- Oversees the execution of Horus’s plans alongside the other traitor commanders.

## 🔗 Connections
- Serves [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lorgar Aurelian]].
- Manipulates [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Horus Lupercal]] and the Sons of Horus.
