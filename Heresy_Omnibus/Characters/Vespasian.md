## 🧠 Summary
A senior commander of the [[Emperor]]’s Children, Vespasian is loyal to the Imperium and deeply concerned about the corruption spreading through his Legion.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]
- Serves as one of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]’s most trusted officers during the Perdus Anomaly campaign.
- Shares suspicions with [[Solomon Demeter]] that the Laer temple has corrupted the Legion.
- Opposes [[Fabius Bile]] and [[Eidolon]]’s excesses, remaining true to the [[Emperor]].
- Killed by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim]] in the Callidenes System after an Ork ambush, removing one of the Legion’s last loyal voices.

## 🔗 Connections
- Ally of [[Solomon Demeter]] and [[Saul Tarvitz]].
- Opposed to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]’s corruption and [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]]’s rebellion.
- Murdered by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]], symbolising the Legion’s fall.
