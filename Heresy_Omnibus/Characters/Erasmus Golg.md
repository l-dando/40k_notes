## 🧠 Summary
Erasmus Golg is a captain of the Iron Warriors, serving under [[Perturabo]] during the Heresy. He commands the *Contrador* at Phall.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novellas/The Crimson Fist|The Crimson Fist]]
- Leads the *Contrador* during the Iron Warriors’ assault on the Retribution Fleet.
- His ship is boarded by [[Alexis Polux]], who kills him and commandeers the vessel.
- His death marks a rare loyalist victory amid the fleet’s retreat.

## 🔗 Connections
- Loyal to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo|Perturabo]].
- Opposed by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Alexis Polux|Alexis Polux]].
