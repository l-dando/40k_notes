## 🧠 Summary
A supporting [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children figure in *[[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Angel Exterminatus|Angel Exterminatus]]*, tied to the Legion’s corrupted expedition under [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim]]. The available lore is brief, but situates Abranxe inside the Legion’s fractured command culture during the hunt for the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Angel Exterminatus|Angel Exterminatus]].
