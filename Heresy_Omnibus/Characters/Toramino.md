## 🧠 Summary
An Iron Warriors Warsmith who served under [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo]] during the joint expedition with the Emperor's Children. Toramino's treachery during the chaos on Iydris — turning his guns on [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Forrix]]'s position — epitomised the internal betrayals and power struggles that fractured even the traitor Legions.

## 🔗 Connections
- Responsible for the death of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Forrix]] by shelling his own side's position.
- Served alongside the Trident under [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo]].
