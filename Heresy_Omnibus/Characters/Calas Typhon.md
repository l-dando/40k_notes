## 🧠 Summary
First Captain of the Death Guard and right hand of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Mortarion]], Typhon is a cunning and ambitious commander who embraces Horus’s rebellion and the powers of Chaos.

## ⚔️ Role in Flight of the Eisenstein
- Commands the *Terminus Est* during the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan III|Isstvan III]] betrayal.
- Attempts to destroy the fleeing *Eisenstein* as it escapes into the Warp.
- His pursuit damages the frigate, stranding it until its rescue by the Imperial Fists.

## 🔗 Connections
- Trusted lieutenant of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Mortarion|Mortarion]].
- Ally of Horus and the traitor Legions.
- Opposed by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Nathaniel Garro]].
