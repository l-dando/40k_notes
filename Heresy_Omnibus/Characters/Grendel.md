## 🧠 Summary
A warrior of the [[Emperor]]'s Children who intervened when [[Eidolon]] attempted to corrupt his battle-brother [[Vull Bronn]] during the Iydris expedition. During the final assault on the Crone World's sepulchre, Grendel destroyed Bombastus, one of the [[Emperor]]'s Children consumed by excess, in the chaos of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]'s attempted ascension.

## 🔗 Connections
- Served in the [[Emperor]]'s Children alongside [[Vull Bronn]].
