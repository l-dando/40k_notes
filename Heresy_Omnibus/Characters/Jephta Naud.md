## 🧠 Summary
General Commander of the military forces of the Interex — a sophisticated, multi-species human civilisation encountered by the 63rd Expeditionary Fleet during the Great Crusade. Naud initially engaged in cautious diplomacy with Warmaster [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Horus Lupercal]], wary of the Imperium's motives. When the Hall of Devices was breached and the Interex suspected the Astartes of theft, Naud led his forces against the 63rd Expedition, leading to the Interex's subjugation.
