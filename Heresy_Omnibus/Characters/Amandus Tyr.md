## 🧠 Summary
Amandus Tyr is a captain of the Imperial Fists, remembered for his boldness and defiance during the battle of Phall.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novellas/The Crimson Fist|The Crimson Fist]]
- Advocates repeatedly for a breakthrough through warp storms, opposing [[Alexis Polux]]’s defensive strategy.
- Boards [[Perturabo]]’s flagship, the *Iron Blood*, in an attempt to kill the Primarch.
- Refuses to withdraw when ordered, and is slain by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo|Perturabo]].

## 🔗 Connections
- Comrade of Polux and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Sigismund|Sigismund]].
- Opposed by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo|Perturabo]].
