## 🧠 Summary
Perturabo is the Primarch of the Iron Warriors Legion, famed for his mastery of siegecraft, fortifications, and relentless pragmatism. Cold, calculating, and embittered, he is a genius engineer and strategist who views war as mathematics and architecture in motion. His bitterness toward the [[Emperor]] and his brothers stems from a lifetime of feeling unappreciated, his talents used but never celebrated. This resentment ultimately drives him to side with [[Horus Lupercal]] during the Heresy.

## ⚔️ Role in the Great Crusade & [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]] Heresy
- Commands the Iron Warriors, shaping them into the Imperium’s foremost siege specialists.
- In *[[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]*, his Legion is among those summoned to [[Isstvan V]], forming part of the second wave that betrays the loyalists during the [[Drop Site Massacre]]. His forces open fire on the remnants of the [[Iron Hands]], Salamanders, and [[Raven Guard]], ensuring their destruction.
- In *[[The Iron Within]]*, his Warsmith [[Barabas Dantioch]] is disgraced after losing a fortress to a Hrud migration, leaving him prematurely aged. Perturabo casts him aside, stripping him of favour. This act indirectly leads to [[Barabas Dantioch|Dantioch]]’s defiance of [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]] and his eventual role in the defence of Terra.
- His wider role in the Heresy is as one of [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]]’s most dependable generals, bringing siege mastery and brutal efficiency to the traitor cause.

## 🔗 Connections
