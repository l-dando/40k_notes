## 🧠 Summary
Alexis Polux is a captain of the Imperial Fists, famed for his resilience and defensive mastery. Rising to command during the Retribution Fleet’s ordeal at Phall, he earns the epithet “[[The Crimson Fist]].”

## ⚔️ Role in [[The Crimson Fist]]
- Dreams of his initiation on Inwit, recalling his twin [[Helias]]’s sacrifice.
- Survives warp storms and assumes command of the Retribution Fleet after Fleet Master Yonnad’s death.
- Holds the fleet in static defence for months despite pressure from [[Amandus Tyr]].
- Faces [[Perturabo]]’s Iron Warriors at Phall, scuttles the *Tribune*, and boards [[Erasmus Golg]]’s *Contrador*, killing Golg and commandeering the ship.
- Emerges from the warp far from Terra, his survival marking him as one of [[Rogal Dorn|Dorn]]’s most trusted captains.

## 🔗 Connections
- Loyal son of [[Rogal Dorn]].
- Comrade of [[Sigismund]] and [[Amandus Tyr]].
- Opposed by [[Perturabo]] and [[Erasmus Golg]].
