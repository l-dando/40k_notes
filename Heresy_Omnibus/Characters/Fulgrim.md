## 🧠 Summary
The Primarch of the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children Legion, Fulgrim is famed for his pursuit of perfection in all things — martial skill, artistry, and the ideals of the Imperium. Radiantly charismatic and impossibly skilled, he inspires fierce loyalty in his warriors. Beneath the flawless exterior lies a pride that will one day make him vulnerable to darker influences.

## ⚔️ Role in the Great Crusade (so far)
- Commands the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children, a Legion devoted to excellence in war and culture.
- In *[[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/False Gods|False Gods]]*, visits the 63rd Expedition during the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Auretian Technocracy|Auretian Technocracy]] campaign.
- Receives the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Items/Anathame|Anathame]] from [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fabius Bile]], who had taken it from the Sons of Horus.
- His presence reinforces the bonds between the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children and Horus’s forces as the Warmaster consolidates allies.

## 🔗 Connections
- Brother‑Primarch to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Horus Lupercal]], with whom he shares mutual respect.
- Served by senior officers such as [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Eidolon]], [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Saul Tarvitz]], and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius]].
- Works closely with [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fabius Bile]], his Chief Apothecary.
