## 🧠 Summary
A supporting Sons of [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]] character in *[[False Gods]]*, appearing in the Legion milieu during [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]]’s corruption and the erosion of internal trust. Tsi Rekh helps complete the cast-level map of officers and lodge-era personnel tied to the pre‑Isstvan betrayal.

## 📖 Book-by-book
### [[False Gods]]
- Appears in the Sons of [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]] supporting cast during the [[Davin]] and Auretian campaign phases.
- Embedded in the same Legion environment shaped by [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]]’s recovery, lodge influence, and anti-loyalist consolidation.
- Serves as continuity/background presence rather than a central protagonist.

## 🔗 Connections
- Part of the same command ecosystem as [[Horus Lupercal]], [[Ezekyle Abaddon]], and [[Maloghurst]].
- Shared book context with [[Tsepha]] and other Sons of [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]] officers.
