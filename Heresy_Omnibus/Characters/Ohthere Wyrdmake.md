## 🧠 Summary
A Rune Priest of the Space Wolves attached to Tra Company, Ohthere Wyrdmake was among the first to examine [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kasper Hawser]] after his recovery on Fenris, noting his inexplicable grasp of the Wolves' languages. As a Rune Priest, Wyrdmake wielded psychic abilities through the Wolves' tradition of rune-reading and seidr, distinct from the formalised sorcery condemned by the Edict of Nikaea.

## 🔗 Connections
- Served alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Amlodhi Skarssen Skarssensson]] in Tra Company.
- One of several Rune Priests who observed and tested [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kasper Hawser]] throughout his time with the Wolves.
