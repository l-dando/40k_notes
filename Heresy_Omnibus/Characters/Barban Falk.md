## 🧠 Summary
Barban Falk was one of the Iron Warriors Trident — the three senior captains of the Iron Warriors alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Forrix]] and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kroeger]], who served as the most trusted commanders under Primarch [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo]]. A disciplined and calculating Warsmith, Falk embodied the Iron Warriors' ethos of methodical siege warfare.
