## 🧠 Summary
Barban Falk was one of the Iron Warriors Trident — the three senior captains of the Iron Warriors alongside [[Forrix]] and [[Kroeger]], who served as the most trusted commanders under Primarch [[Perturabo]]. A disciplined and calculating Warsmith, Falk embodied the Iron Warriors' ethos of methodical siege warfare.

## 🔗 Connections
- One of the three Triarchs; fought alongside [[Forrix]] and [[Kroeger]].
- Served directly under [[Perturabo]].
