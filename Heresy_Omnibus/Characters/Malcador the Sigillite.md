## 🧠 Summary
An ancient and immensely powerful human psyker, second only to the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]. Malcador serves as the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s regent, trusted advisor, and political instrument during the Great Crusade.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/The First Heretic|The First Heretic]]
- Present at [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Monarchia|Monarchia]] after its destruction by the Ultramarines.
- Attempts to explain the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s censure to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lorgar Aurelian]], but is struck to the ground.
- Psychically summons the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]] to the surface to confront Lorgar.

## 🔗 Connections
- Close confidant of the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]].
- Works alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Roboute Guilliman]] during the censure of the Word Bearers.
