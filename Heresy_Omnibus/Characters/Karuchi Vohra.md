## 🧠 Summary
An Aeldari guide who accompanied [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim]] and the Emperor's Children on the expedition to the Crone World of Iydris. Vohra served as a guide through the perilous webway paths and haunted ruins of the dead Aeldari world, navigating terrain that was lethal even to Astartes. His motives — and whether he served Fulgrim willingly or under compulsion — remained ambiguous throughout the campaign.

## 🔗 Connections
- Guided [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim]] and the Emperor's Children to Iydris.
- Introduced to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo]] and the Iron Warriors by Fulgrim as a resource for the expedition.
