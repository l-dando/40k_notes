## 🧠 Summary
An Aeldari guide who accompanied [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim]] and the [[Emperor]]'s Children on the expedition to the Crone World of Iydris. Vohra served as a guide through the perilous webway paths and haunted ruins of the dead Aeldari world, navigating terrain that was lethal even to Astartes. His motives — and whether he served [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]] willingly or under compulsion — remained ambiguous throughout the campaign.
