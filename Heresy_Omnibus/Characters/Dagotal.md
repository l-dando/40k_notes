## 🧠 Summary
A veteran sergeant in [[Argel Tal]]’s Seventh Assault Company, later one of the possessed Gal Vorbak.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/The First Heretic|The First Heretic]]
- Escorts [[Cyrene Valantion]] through the City of Grey Flowers on Colchis.
- Accompanies [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Argel Tal|Argel Tal]] into the Eye of Terror aboard the *Orfeo’s Lament*.
- Survives possession and returns as part of the Gal Vorbak.
- Killed at [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan V|Isstvan V]], incinerated by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Items/Raven Guard|Raven Guard]] flamers during the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Events/Drop Site Massacre|Drop Site Massacre]].

## 🔗 Connections
- Comrade of [[Malnor]], [[Torgal]], and [[Xaphen]].
- Loyal to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Argel Tal|Argel Tal]].
