## 🧠 Summary
A loyalist Astartes serving with the Shattered Legions aboard the *Sisypheum* during the [[Angel Exterminatus]] campaign. Cassander attempted to kill [[Fabius Bile]] in the [[Emperor]]'s Children's apothecarion, but was instead captured and transformed by Bile into a terata — a monstrous, corrupted creature. His fate served as a grim warning to the other loyalists of the dangers of confronting the traitors' most twisted practitioner directly.

## 🔗 Connections
- Served alongside [[Nykona Sharrowkyn]] and [[Sabik Wayland]] in the Shattered Legions.
