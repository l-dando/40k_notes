## 🧠 Summary
Captain of the Catulan Reaver Squad — one of the elite assault units of the Luna Wolves. Ekaddon was a capable and brutal warrior, representative of the more aggressive elements within the Legion. He followed the Legion into the Heresy, serving the Sons of Horus.
