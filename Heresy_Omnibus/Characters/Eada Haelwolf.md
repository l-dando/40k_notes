## 🧠 Summary
A Rune Priest of the Space Wolves who attempted to break into [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kasper Hawser]]'s mind to expose any hidden manipulation, but instead became possessed. In the violent confrontation that followed, Haelwolf transformed into a monstrous shadow-wolf and was slain. His dying words warned of a plot against [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Horus Lupercal]], though the warning was dismissed by his commander [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Amlodhi Skarssen Skarssensson]].
