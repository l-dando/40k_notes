## 🧠 Summary
A Rune Priest of the Space Wolves who attempted to break into [[Kasper Hawser]]'s mind to expose any hidden manipulation, but instead became possessed. In the violent confrontation that followed, Haelwolf transformed into a monstrous shadow-wolf and was slain. His dying words warned of a plot against [[Horus Lupercal]], though the warning was dismissed by his commander [[Amlodhi Skarssen Skarssensson]].

## 🔗 Connections
- Killed while attempting to psychically examine [[Kasper Hawser]].
- Served in the Space Wolves alongside [[Aun Helwintr]] and [[Ohthere Wyrdmake]].
