## 🧠 Summary
Architect Designate assigned to the 63rd Expeditionary Fleet as part of the Remembrancer Order. Momus was responsible for designing and overseeing the construction of Imperial monuments and settlement infrastructure on worlds brought into compliance, commemorating the Great Crusade's conquests in stone and metal.
