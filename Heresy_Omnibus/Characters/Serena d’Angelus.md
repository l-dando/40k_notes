## 🧠 Summary
A painter obsessed with sensation and perfection, d’Angelus becomes increasingly corrupted by Slaaneshi influence during the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children campaign.

## ⚔️ Role in Fulgrim
- Attends [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Bequa Kynska|Bequa Kynska]]’s recital aboard the *Pride of the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]*.
- Obsessed with the Laer temple, begins painting with blood to overcome her artistic block.
- Creates a distorted portrait of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]], reflecting his corruption.
- Confesses her murder of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Leopold Cadmus]] to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius]], inspiring his self‑scarification.
- Finds [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Ostian Delafour]]’s corpse and, overcome with grief, commits suicide.

## 🔗 Connections
- Rival and counterpart to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Ostian Delafour|Ostian Delafour]].
- Influences [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius|Lucius]]’s descent into excess.
