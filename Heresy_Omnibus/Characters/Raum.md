## 🧠 Summary
A daemon bound within [[Argel Tal]] during the events of the Pilgrimage, sharing his body and mind.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/The First Heretic|The First Heretic]]
- Possesses [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Argel Tal|Argel Tal]] during the Gal Vorbak’s creation in the Eye of Terror.
- Speaks to Tal openly for the first time before the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Events/Drop Site Massacre|Drop Site Massacre]], prophesying they will “die in the shadow of great wings.”
- Battles Tal for control after the death of [[Cyrene Valantion]].
- Declares himself Tal’s “brother” after [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan V|Isstvan V]].

## 🔗 Connections
