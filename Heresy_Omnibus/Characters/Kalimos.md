## 🧠 Summary
An Iron Warriors supporting character in *[[Angel Exterminatus]]*, tied to the Legion’s brutal chain of command during [[Perturabo]]’s campaign. Kalimos helps document the wider officer corps active around the Iron Warriors’ siege-driven operations.

## 📖 Book-by-book
### [[Angel Exterminatus]]
- Appears among Iron Warriors personnel in the Hydra Cordatus and Iydris campaign arc.
- Contributes to the novel’s depiction of attritional strategy, internal rivalry, and hardline discipline.
- Remains a background figure without a standalone narrative arc.

## 🔗 Connections
- Operates under the wider strategic command of [[Perturabo]].
- Linked in-campaign with [[Berossus]], [[Kroeger]], and [[Obax Zakayo]].
