## 🧠 Summary
Sigismund is the First Captain of the Imperial Fists, renowned for his martial skill and later as the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Champion. In *[[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novellas/The Crimson Fist|The Crimson Fist]]*, his crisis of faith and loyalty to Dorn is revealed.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novellas/The Crimson Fist|The Crimson Fist]]
- Relieved himself of command of the Retribution Fleet before departure.
- Confesses to [[Rogal Dorn]] that he remained on Terra because [[Euphrati Keeler]] warned him his Primarch would need him, otherwise he would die at Phall.
- Dorn, furious, disowns him but orders him to continue as if nothing had changed.

## 🔗 Connections
- First Captain under [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Rogal Dorn|Rogal Dorn]].
- Guided by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Euphrati Keeler|Euphrati Keeler]]’s visions.
- Comrade of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Alexis Polux|Alexis Polux]].
