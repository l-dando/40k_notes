## 🧠 Summary
Sigismund is the First Captain of the Imperial Fists, renowned for his martial skill and later as the [[Emperor]]’s Champion. In *[[The Crimson Fist]]*, his crisis of faith and loyalty to [[Rogal Dorn|Dorn]] is revealed.

## ⚔️ Role in [[The Crimson Fist]]
- Relieved himself of command of the Retribution Fleet before departure.
- Confesses to [[Rogal Dorn]] that he remained on Terra because [[Euphrati Keeler]] warned him his Primarch would need him, otherwise he would die at Phall.
- [[Rogal Dorn|Dorn]], furious, disowns him but orders him to continue as if nothing had changed.

## 🔗 Connections
- First Captain under [[Rogal Dorn]].
- Guided by [[Euphrati Keeler]]’s visions.
- Comrade of [[Alexis Polux]].
