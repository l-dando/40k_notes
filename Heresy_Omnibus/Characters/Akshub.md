## 🧠 Summary
A Chaos cult priestess of the Serpent Lodge on [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Davin|Davin]], instrumental in the ritual to heal Horus.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/False Gods|False Gods]]
- Oversees the ritual to send [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Erebus]] into the spirit realm.
- Slits [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Erebus|Erebus]]’s throat as part of the rite.

## 🔗 Connections
- Serves the Serpent Lodge and the Ruinous Powers.
- Works alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Erebus]] in Horus’s corruption.
