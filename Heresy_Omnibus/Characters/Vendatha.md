## 🧠 Summary
A Shield-Captain of the Custodes assigned to monitor the Word Bearers during the Pilgrimage.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/The First Heretic|The First Heretic]]
- Accompanies the 1,301st Expedition into the Cadian system.
- Opposes [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lorgar Aurelian]]’s participation in the tribal ritual on [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Cadia|Cadia]].
- Kills [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Deumos|Deumos]] and two other Word Bearers before being mortally wounded by [[Xaphen]] and [[Argel Tal]].
- Used as the tenth sacrifice in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Ingethel|Ingethel]]’s ritual.

## 🔗 Connections
- Serves under [[Aquillon]].
- Watches over the Word Bearers on the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s orders.
