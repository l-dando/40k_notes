## 🧠 Summary
A master swordsman of the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children, famed for his skill and vanity. Holds himself to impossibly high standards of martial perfection.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Horus Rising|Horus Rising]]
- Fights alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Saul Tarvitz]] on Murder.
- Displays disdain for those he considers less skilled.
- Survives the virus‑bombing on [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan III|Isstvan III]] thanks to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Saul Tarvitz]]’s warning.
- Grows jealous of Tarvitz’s leadership during the loyalist defence.
- Murders Chaplain [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Charmosian]] and betrays the loyalists to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Eidolon]] in exchange for a place among the traitors.
- Duels Tarvitz but escapes after being wounded.

## 🔗 Connections
- Rival and comrade to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Saul Tarvitz]].
- Serves under [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Eidolon]].
