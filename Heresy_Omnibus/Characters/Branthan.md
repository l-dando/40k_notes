## 🧠 Summary
A Salamanders legionary serving with the Shattered Legions aboard the *Sisypheum*. Branthan was accompanied by a psyber-eagle — a mechadendrite-linked avian familiar — which proved pivotal in the climax of the Iydris campaign. When [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim]] prepared to sacrifice [[Perturabo]] using a gemstone draining his life force, Branthan's psyber-eagle snatched the hurled artefact, enabling [[Perturabo]] to strike back against [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]].

## 🔗 Connections
- Served alongside [[Nykona Sharrowkyn]], [[Sabik Wayland]], and other Shattered Legions survivors.
- His psyber-eagle was crucial to defeating [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]'s plan on Iydris.
