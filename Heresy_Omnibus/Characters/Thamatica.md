## 🧠 Summary
A Techmarine serving with the loyalist Shattered Legions aboard the *Sisypheum*. Thamatica's experiments in attempting to locate and oppose the traitors ultimately exposed the *Sisypheum*'s position to the enemy. In the desperate escape from Iydris, Thamatica overcharged the ship's reactors, sending the *Sisypheum* crashing through the Emperor's Children flagship in a final act of defiance.
