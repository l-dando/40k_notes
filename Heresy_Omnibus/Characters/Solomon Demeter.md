## 🧠 Summary
An honourable and loyal captain of the [[Emperor]]’s Children, Demeter resists corruption longer than most of his Legion.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]
- Recovers from wounds sustained against the Laer.
- Suspects corruption spreading through the Legion after the Laer campaign.
- Saved from an Ork ambush by [[Saul Tarvitz]].
- Betrayed by [[Lucius]], tricked into killing his own men.
- Dies from a sword wound, abandoned by [[Lucius]].

## 🔗 Connections
- Ally of [[Saul Tarvitz|Tarvitz]] and [[Vespasian]].
- Opposed to Fabius and [[Eidolon]]’s excesses.
