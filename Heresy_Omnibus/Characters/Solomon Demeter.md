## 🧠 Summary
An honourable and loyal captain of the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children, Demeter resists corruption longer than most of his Legion.

## ⚔️ Role in Fulgrim
- Recovers from wounds sustained against the Laer.
- Suspects corruption spreading through the Legion after the Laer campaign.
- Saved from an Ork ambush by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Saul Tarvitz]].
- Betrayed by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius]], tricked into killing his own men.
- Dies from a sword wound, abandoned by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius|Lucius]].

## 🔗 Connections
- Ally of Tarvitz and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Vespasian|Vespasian]].
- Opposed to Fabius and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Eidolon|Eidolon]]’s excesses.
