## 🧠 Summary
A pragmatic and honourable Captain of the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children, respected for his tactical sense and moral compass.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Horus Rising|Horus Rising]]
- Commands forces on Murder alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius]].
- Orders the destruction of Megarachnid “trees,” clearing storms and enabling Imperial victory.
- Forms a bond with [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Tarik Torgaddon]].
- Discovers Horus’s plan to virus‑bomb [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan III|Isstvan III]] and warns the loyalists.
- Emerges as the de facto leader of the loyalist defence.
- Duels [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius]] after discovering his betrayal; wounds him but cannot kill him.
- Survives until the final orbital bombardment, resolved to fight to the end.

## 🔗 Connections
- Ally of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Tarik Torgaddon]].
- Often at odds with [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Eidolon]]’s arrogance.
