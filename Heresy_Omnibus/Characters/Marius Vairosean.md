## 🧠 Summary
A captain of the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children whose corruption leads him to become the first of the Noise Marines.

## ⚔️ Role in Fulgrim
- Takes up a strange instrument created by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Bequa Kynska]].
- Transformed into the first Noise Marine during the *Maraviglia* performance.
- Leads others into excess and mutation under Slaanesh’s influence.

## 🔗 Connections
- Ally of Kynska.
- Serves Fulgrim’s corrupted vision.
