## 🧠 Summary
A captain of the [[Emperor]]’s Children whose corruption leads him to become the first of the Noise Marines.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]
- Takes up a strange instrument created by [[Bequa Kynska]].
- Transformed into the first Noise Marine during the *Maraviglia* performance.
- Leads others into excess and mutation under Slaanesh’s influence.

## 🔗 Connections
- Ally of Kynska.
- Serves [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]’s corrupted vision.
