## 🧠 Summary
An Imperial Army supporting figure in *Fulgrim*, representing the human military institutions operating alongside the Legiones Astartes. Fayle’s footprint is limited, but helps anchor the novel’s wider expeditionary context beyond Primarch and Legion command circles.

## 📖 Book-by-book
### [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]
- Appears in the human military/expedition support cast around the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children campaign thread.
- Reflects the Imperial Army presence that persists even as Legion politics and Chaos influence escalate.
- Not developed as a principal narrative character.

## 🔗 Connections
- Part of the same non‑Astartes military context as [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Coraline Asenec]] and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Evander Tobias]].
