## 🧠 Summary
Forrix was the First Captain of the Iron Warriors and one of the Trident — the three senior captains who served as [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo]]'s closest commanders alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Barban Falk]] and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kroeger]]. A veteran of countless sieges, Forrix was pragmatic and tactically brilliant, embodying the Iron Warriors' grinding, methodical approach to warfare. He was killed on the Crone World of Iydris when [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Toramino]] turned his guns on his own position during the chaos of Fulgrim's betrayal.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Angel Exterminatus|Angel Exterminatus]]
- One of the three Triarchs commanding the Iron Warriors alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo|Perturabo]] during the joint expedition with the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]'s Children.
- Warned by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo|Perturabo]] not to trust Fulgrim or the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]'s Children.
- Killed by friendly fire when [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Toramino|Toramino]] shelled his position during the internal Iron Warriors power struggle.
