## 🧠 Summary
Forrix was the First Captain of the Iron Warriors and one of the Trident — the three senior captains who served as [[Perturabo]]'s closest commanders alongside [[Barban Falk]] and [[Kroeger]]. A veteran of countless sieges, Forrix was pragmatic and tactically brilliant, embodying the Iron Warriors' grinding, methodical approach to warfare. He was killed on the Crone World of Iydris when [[Toramino]] turned his guns on his own position during the chaos of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]'s betrayal.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Angel Exterminatus]]
- One of the three Triarchs commanding the Iron Warriors alongside [[Perturabo]] during the joint expedition with the [[Emperor]]'s Children.
- Warned by [[Perturabo]] not to trust [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]] or the [[Emperor]]'s Children.
- Killed by friendly fire when [[Toramino]] shelled his position during the internal Iron Warriors power struggle.

## 🔗 Connections
- Fellow Triarch alongside [[Barban Falk]] and [[Kroeger]].
- Loyally served [[Perturabo]].
