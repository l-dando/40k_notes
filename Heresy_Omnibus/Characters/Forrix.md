## 🧠 Summary
Forrix was the First Captain of the Iron Warriors and one of the Trident — the three senior captains who served as [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Perturabo]]'s closest commanders alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Barban Falk]] and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kroeger]]. A veteran of countless sieges, Forrix was pragmatic and tactically brilliant, embodying the Iron Warriors' grinding, methodical approach to warfare. He was killed on the Crone World of Iydris when [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Toramino]] turned his guns on his own position during the chaos of Fulgrim's betrayal.

## ⚔️ Role in Angel Exterminatus
- One of the three Triarchs commanding the Iron Warriors alongside Perturabo during the joint expedition with the Emperor's Children.
- Warned by Perturabo not to trust Fulgrim or the Emperor's Children.
- Killed by friendly fire when Toramino shelled his position during the internal Iron Warriors power struggle.
