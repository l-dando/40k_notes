## 🧠 Summary
An ambitious and arrogant Lord Commander of the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children. Known for his pride in perfection and disdain for those he considers beneath him.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Horus Rising|Horus Rising]]
- Leads the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children forces on Murder.
- Ignores protocol and drops into battle without waiting for the Luna Wolves.
- Clashes with [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Saul Tarvitz]] over tactics.
- Uses a surgically‑implanted sonic weapon to kill a Warsinger on Isstvan Extremis.
- Leads traitor assaults on loyalist positions during the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan III|Isstvan III]] ground war.
- Massacres loyalist wounded in the palace before being repelled by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Saul Tarvitz]]’s forces.

## 🔗 Connections
- Superior to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Saul Tarvitz]] and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lucius]].
- Respected by some for his skill, but criticised for arrogance.
