## 🧠 Summary
A Rune Priest of the Space Wolves who died defending [[Kasper Hawser]] during the assault on the Olamic Quietude. In a shared dream-state as he lay dying, Heoroth showed [[Kasper Hawser|Hawser]] a vision of a great black wolf — the reincarnated spirit of Brom, an Ascomanni warrior who had died protecting [[Kasper Hawser|Hawser]] on Fenris. Heoroth's sacrifice marked [[Kasper Hawser|Hawser]] deeply, and his death was mourned by the Wolves in the tradition of their Legion.
