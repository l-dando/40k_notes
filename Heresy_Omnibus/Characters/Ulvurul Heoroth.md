## 🧠 Summary
A Rune Priest of the Space Wolves who died defending [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kasper Hawser]] during the assault on the Olamic Quietude. In a shared dream-state as he lay dying, Heoroth showed Hawser a vision of a great black wolf — the reincarnated spirit of Brom, an Ascomanni warrior who had died protecting Hawser on Fenris. Heoroth's sacrifice marked Hawser deeply, and his death was mourned by the Wolves in the tradition of their Legion.
