## 🧠 Summary
Equerry and personal aide to Warmaster [[Horus Lupercal]], Maloghurst served as his master's voice and administrative right hand throughout the Great Crusade. Crippled in battle and known as "the Twisted" for his broken gait, he was nonetheless one of the most politically sharp minds in the 63rd Expedition. He managed the [[Mournival]], coordinated lodge politics, and became one of the most loyal servants of [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]]'s rebellion.

## ⚔️ Role in the Heresy
- Administrated the 63rd Expedition Fleet's political operations on behalf of the Warmaster.
- Played a key role in managing the Warrior Lodge network within the Sons of [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]].
- Oversaw the transition from the Luna Wolves to the Sons of [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]] name.

## 🔗 Connections
- Personally loyal to [[Horus Lupercal]] above all else.
- Interacted regularly with the [[Mournival]]: [[Ezekyle Abaddon]], [[Tarik Torgaddon]], [[Horus Aximand]], and [[Garviel Loken]].
