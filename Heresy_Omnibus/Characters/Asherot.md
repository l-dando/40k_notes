## 🧠 Summary
An indentured Kinebrach — a xenos species allied with the Interex — serving as Keeper of Devices in the Hall of Devices on the Interex homeworld. The Kinebrach were weapon-smiths of great skill, and Asherot guarded the museum of dangerous xenos artefacts that the Interex had collected and contained, including the [[Anathame]]. When the Hall was breached and the [[Anathame]] stolen (in truth by [[Erebus]]), the Interex blamed the Astartes, triggering the conflict that destroyed their civilisation.
