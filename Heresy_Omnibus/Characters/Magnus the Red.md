## 🧠 Summary
The cyclopean Primarch of the Thousand Sons Legion, unmatched in psychic mastery among his brothers.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/The First Heretic|The First Heretic]]
- Visits [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Lorgar Aurelian]] on Colchis after the censure at [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Monarchia|Monarchia]].
- Evades Lorgar’s questions about the existence of gods in the Warp.
- Later appears within [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Horus Lupercal]]’s vision in the Serpent Lodge ([[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/False Gods|False Gods]]), warning him of manipulation.

## 🔗 Connections
- Ally and occasional confidant of Lorgar.
- Opposes the worship of the Chaos Gods.
