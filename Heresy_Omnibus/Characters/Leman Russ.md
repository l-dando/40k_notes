## 🧠 Summary
Leman Russ, the Wolf King, was the Primarch of the VI Legion — the Space Wolves. Raised on the feral world of Fenris by a she-wolf, he embodied the savage nobility of his adoptive world, balancing bestial ferocity with fierce loyalty to the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]. Among the most powerful warriors of the Primarchs, Russ wielded Mjalnar, his frost blade, and the axe Helwinter with devastating effect.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Prospero Burns|Prospero Burns]]
- Led the Space Wolves' assault on the planet Prospero under the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]'s direct sanction, tasked with bringing [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Magnus the Red]] to Terra to answer for his transgressions against the Edict of Nikaea.
- Used [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kasper Hawser]] as a psychic conduit to offer Magnus a final chance to surrender before the attack.
- Interrogated Hawser personally at multiple points, suspecting the Wolves had been manipulated.
