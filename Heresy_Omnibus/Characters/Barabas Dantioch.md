## 🧠 Summary
A Warsmith of the Iron Warriors, Dantioch fell from favour with [[Perturabo]] after losing a fortress to a Hrud migration that left him prematurely aged and weakened. Despite his disgrace, he remained loyal to the [[Emperor]] during the [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]] Heresy.

## ⚔️ Role in [[The Iron Within]]
- Commands the Schadenhold fortress on Lesser Damantyne.
- Refuses [[Idriss Krendl]]’s summons to join [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]], declaring loyalty to the [[Emperor]].
- Supported by [[Vastopol]] and aided by [[Tauro Nicodemus]], defends the Schadenhold for a year against traitor forces.
- Executes a contingency plan, collapsing the fortress onto traitor Titans and teleporting his allies onto Krendl’s flagship.
- Escapes and accepts Nicodemus’s offer to travel to Terra, lending his expertise to the defences of the Imperial Palace.

## 🔗 Connections
- Former commander of [[Idriss Krendl]].
- Ally of [[Vastopol]] and [[Tauro Nicodemus]].
- Loyalist Iron Warrior who redeems his honour through defiance.
