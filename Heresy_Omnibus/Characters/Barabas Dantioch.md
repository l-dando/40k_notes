## 🧠 Summary
A Warsmith of the Iron Warriors, Dantioch fell from favour with [[Perturabo]] after losing a fortress to a Hrud migration that left him prematurely aged and weakened. Despite his disgrace, he remained loyal to the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]] during the Horus Heresy.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Short Stories/The Iron Within|The Iron Within]]
- Commands the Schadenhold fortress on Lesser Damantyne.
- Refuses [[Idriss Krendl]]’s summons to join Horus, declaring loyalty to the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]].
- Supported by [[Vastopol]] and aided by [[Tauro Nicodemus]], defends the Schadenhold for a year against traitor forces.
- Executes a contingency plan, collapsing the fortress onto traitor Titans and teleporting his allies onto Krendl’s flagship.
- Escapes and accepts Nicodemus’s offer to travel to Terra, lending his expertise to the defences of the Imperial Palace.

## 🔗 Connections
- Former commander of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Idriss Krendl|Idriss Krendl]].
- Ally of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Vastopol|Vastopol]] and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Tauro Nicodemus|Tauro Nicodemus]].
- Loyalist Iron Warrior who redeems his honour through defiance.
