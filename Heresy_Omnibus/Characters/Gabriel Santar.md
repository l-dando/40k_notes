## 🧠 Summary
First Captain of the Iron Hands Legion and personal equerry to Primarch [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Ferrus Manus]], Gabriel Santar was among the most trusted of the Gorgon's warriors. Utterly devoted to his Primarch, he served as both battlefield commander and loyal aide throughout the Great Crusade's later stages.

## ⚔️ Role in the Heresy
- Served alongside [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Ferrus Manus]] during the joint campaigns with the Emperor's Children in the Perdus Anomaly.
- Present at the Isstvan V Drop Site Massacre, where he died alongside many of the Iron Hands' finest warriors in the traitors' ambush.

## 🔗 Connections
- Utterly loyal to [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Ferrus Manus]].
