## 🧠 Summary
Originally one of the Ascomanni tribe who sheltered [[Kasper Hawser]] after his crash on Fenris, Fith was one of two survivors rescued alongside [[Kasper Hawser|Hawser]] by [[Bjorn]]. He later joined the Space Wolves, becoming a full Legionary under the name Fith Godsmote. During [[Kasper Hawser|Hawser]]'s time with the Wolves, Fith taught him axework and became a familiar face across decades of service.

## 🔗 Connections
- Originally protected [[Kasper Hawser]] alongside Brom before the Space Wolves' arrival.
- Served under [[Amlodhi Skarssen Skarssensson]] in Tra Company.
