## 🧠 Summary
Originally one of the Ascomanni tribe who sheltered [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kasper Hawser]] after his crash on Fenris, Fith was one of two survivors rescued alongside Hawser by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Bjorn]]. He later joined the Space Wolves, becoming a full Legionary under the name Fith Godsmote. During Hawser's time with the Wolves, Fith taught him axework and became a familiar face across decades of service.
