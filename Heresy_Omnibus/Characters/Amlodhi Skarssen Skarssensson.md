## 🧠 Summary
Captain of Tra — the Third Company of the Space Wolves — and the commanding officer under whose banner [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kasper Hawser]] served as skjald. Amlodhi Skarssen Skarssensson was a veteran warrior of the VI Legion, leading his pack through the Great Crusade and the eventual assault on Prospero.

## 🔗 Connections
- Commanded [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Bjorn]], [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fith Godsmote]], and other warriors of Tra Company.
- The company's deeds were chronicled by [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kasper Hawser]].
