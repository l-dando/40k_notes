## 🧠 Summary
A champion of the Ultramarines and one of the Tetrarchs of Ultramar, Nicodemus disguises himself as a clerk to warn [[Barabas Dantioch]] of [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]]’s treachery. He plays a pivotal role in the defence of the Schadenhold.

## ⚔️ Role in [[The Iron Within]]
- Reveals his identity after Krendl’s failed attempt to recruit [[Barabas Dantioch|Dantioch]].
- Vows to aid [[Barabas Dantioch|Dantioch]] in the coming siege.
- Fights alongside [[Barabas Dantioch|Dantioch]] and [[Vastopol]] during the year‑long defence.
- Escapes with [[Barabas Dantioch|Dantioch]] after the fortress collapses, seizing Krendl’s flagship.
- Offers [[Barabas Dantioch|Dantioch]] a new purpose: to travel to Terra and help fortify the Imperial Palace.

## 🔗 Connections
- Ally of [[Barabas Dantioch|Dantioch]] and [[Vastopol]].
- Opposes Krendl and the traitor Iron Warriors.
- Representative of Ultramar’s loyalty to the Imperium.
