## 🧠 Summary
A powerful sorcerer and senior member of the Thousand Sons, Amon served as one of the closest aides to Primarch [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Magnus the Red]]. He was directly involved in the covert manipulation of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kasper Hawser]] — a plan to plant a deep-cover agent inside the Space Wolves. At the Council of Nikaea, a Chaos entity impersonated Amon to frame the Thousand Sons, helping engineer the conflict that led to the Burning of Prospero. The real Amon appeared at Nikaea to confirm the truth to Hawser before being driven off by the Wolves.
