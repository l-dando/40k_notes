## 🧠 Summary
A Moderati aboard the Warlord‑class Titan *Dies Irae*, serving under Princeps [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Esau Turnet]] during the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan III|Isstvan III]] campaign.

## ⚔️ Role in [[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Galaxy in Flames|Galaxy in Flames]]
- Friend of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Titus Cassar]], though less devout in faith.
- Survives the virus‑bombing and firestorm thanks to Turnet’s forewarning.
- Chooses ambition over loyalty, killing Cassar when he realises Cassar’s devotion to the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]].
