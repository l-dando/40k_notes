## 🧠 Summary
First Captain and Chapter Master of the Blood Angels, Raldoron was one of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Sanguinius]]'s most trusted lieutenants. He attended the Council of Nikaea alongside his Primarch, and served as a senior voice of the IX Legion throughout the Great Crusade.

## 🔗 Connections
- Direct subordinate and right hand of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Sanguinius]].
- Present at the Council of Nikaea with other senior Astartes and Primarchs.
