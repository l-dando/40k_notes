## 🧠 Summary
Subordinate Commander of the military forces of the Interex, second to [[Jephta Naud]]. Tull served during the tense first contact between the Interex and the 63rd Expeditionary Fleet, and fought in the conflict that followed the theft of the [[Anathame]] from the Hall of Devices.

## 🔗 Connections
- Served under [[Jephta Naud]] in the Interex military.
