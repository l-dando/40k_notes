## 🧭 Overview
*Lord of the Red Sands* takes place during the brutal fighting on [[Isstvan III]], where [[Angron]] leads the traitor World Eaters against the loyalist remnants of their own Legion, as well as those of the [[Emperor]]’s Children and Death Guard. The story captures [[Angron]]’s ferocity and philosophy, contrasting his savagery with a rare moment of grim compassion.

Rampaging across the battlefield, [[Angron]] slaughters countless loyalists until confronted by his own Captain, [[Kauragar]]. In a final act of defiance, [[Kauragar]] manages to wound his gene‑sire’s throat — a feat few have ever achieved — before being struck down. Pleased by his son’s courage, [[Angron]] remains with him as he dies. With his last breath, [[Kauragar]] asks why [[Angron]] now fights for [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]]. The Primarch replies that he does not: on Nuceria he learned that the only cause worth fighting for is freedom. In siding with [[Horus Lupercal|Horus]], [[Angron]] believes he has cast off the [[Emperor]]’s chains and become a free man at last.

## 🔗 Connections
- Set during the [[Isstvan III]] betrayal, alongside events of *[[Galaxy in Flames]]*.
- Features [[Angron]] and his World Eaters, highlighting their descent into fratricidal slaughter.
- Introduces Captain [[Kauragar]], whose death underscores the tragedy of the Legion’s fall.
