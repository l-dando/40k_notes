## 🧭 Overview
*Lord of the Red Sands* takes place during the brutal fighting on [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan III|Isstvan III]], where [[Angron]] leads the traitor World Eaters against the loyalist remnants of their own Legion, as well as those of the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s Children and Death Guard. The story captures [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Angron|Angron]]’s ferocity and philosophy, contrasting his savagery with a rare moment of grim compassion.

Rampaging across the battlefield, [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Angron|Angron]] slaughters countless loyalists until confronted by his own Captain, [[Kauragar]]. In a final act of defiance, [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kauragar|Kauragar]] manages to wound his gene‑sire’s throat — a feat few have ever achieved — before being struck down. Pleased by his son’s courage, [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Angron|Angron]] remains with him as he dies. With his last breath, [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Kauragar|Kauragar]] asks why [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Angron|Angron]] now fights for Horus. The Primarch replies that he does not: on Nuceria he learned that the only cause worth fighting for is freedom. In siding with Horus, [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Angron|Angron]] believes he has cast off the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]’s chains and become a free man at last.

## 🔗 Connections
- Set during the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Places/Isstvan III|Isstvan III]] betrayal, alongside events of *[[Heresy_Omnibus/Books/Novels/Galaxy in Flames|Galaxy in Flames]]*.
- Features [[Angron]] and his World Eaters, highlighting their descent into fratricidal slaughter.
- Introduces Captain [[Kauragar]], whose death underscores the tragedy of the Legion’s fall.
