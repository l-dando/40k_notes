# 🧭 Overview
A hastily assembled loyalist strike force races to intercept the *Furious Abyss*, a colossal Word Bearers warship built to annihilate Macragge before open war is fully declared. Drawn from multiple Legions and burdened by mistrust, the crew must cooperate under impossible pressure to stop a single mission from changing the balance of the Heresy.

The novel highlights void warfare, inter‑Legion tensions, and the increasingly covert escalation of Horus's rebellion.
