# 🧭 Overview

---

## Book One — Terra Figula
[[Perturabo]]’s earliest memory is of climbing a cliff on Olympia, foreshadowing a lifetime of struggle and defiance. In the present, the Iron Warriors besiege the Cadmean Citadel on Hydra Cordatus. After months of grinding warfare, [[Perturabo]] personally intervenes to break the Imperial Fists’ defence, promoting the brutal [[Kroeger]] and demoting the reckless [[Harkor]].

The [[Emperor]]’s Children arrive unexpectedly, shocking the Iron Warriors with their mutated decadence. [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]] introduces an Aeldari guide, [[Karuchi Vohra]], and demands [[Perturabo]] construct the Thaliakron so he can recount the legend of the Angel Exterminatus — a weapon of unimaginable power. Hidden Shattered Legion operatives, [[Sabik Wayland]] and [[Nykona Sharrowkyn]], attempt to assassinate [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]], but he survives.

[[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]] reveals his true goal: to enter a warp rift and claim the Angel Exterminatus. [[Perturabo]] reluctantly agrees, warning the Trident ([[Forrix]], Falk, [[Kroeger]]) not to trust the [[Emperor]]’s Children. Meanwhile, [[Fabius Bile]] experiments on captured Imperial Fists, and [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]] secretly plots to resurrect the Angel Exterminatus for himself.

---

## Book Two — As Above, So Below
On Medusa IV, a vision of [[Ferrus Manus]] hunts a wyrm, symbolising the lingering trauma of [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]’s fratricide.

[[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]], haunted by memories, reconvenes the Brotherhood of the Phoenix. On the Sisypheum, the Shattered Legions prepare to oppose the traitors. [[Cassander]] attempts to kill Fabius but is transformed into a terata monstrosity. The loyalists navigate the Paths Below while the traitors approach the warp rift — now named the **Eye of Terror** by [[Perturabo]].

Tensions rise within both Legions. [[Eidolon]] attempts to corrupt [[Vull Bronn]], [[Grendel]] intervenes, and [[Thamatica]]’s experiments expose the Sisypheum’s location. [[Perturabo]] boards the loyalist ship, slaughtering defenders while Fabius raids the apothecarion. A catastrophic energy spike forces the Iron Warriors to evacuate their Primarch. [[Thamatica]] overcharges the Sisypheum’s reactors, sending it crashing through the [[Emperor]]’s Children flagship. [[Perturabo]] allows the loyalists to escape as punishment for [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]’s deceit.

[[Perturabo]] confronts [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]], asserting command of the expedition.

---

## Book Three — Coniunctio
On Chemos, [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]’s origin is retold: a miraculous child found in a polluted wasteland, destined for greatness.

The traitors reach the Crone World Iydris, whose very landscape resists them. As they advance through the haunted sepulchre, the [[Emperor]]’s Children succumb to sensory madness while Aeldari spirits rise to defend their dead world. Shattered Legion survivors infiltrate the complex from another route.

[[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]] reveals his true plan: to sacrifice [[Perturabo]] and ascend as the Angel Exterminatus. A gemstone gifted to [[Perturabo]] secretly drains his life. The Iron Warriors fracture under internal betrayal as [[Toramino]] shells [[Forrix]]’s position, and [[Kroeger]] kills [[Harkor]] in a frenzy.

[[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]] rises through the sepulchre’s core, leaving [[Perturabo]] dying. [[Eidolon]] begins the ritual sacrifice — but [[Perturabo]] seizes the gemstone and confronts [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]] in a vision of an Olympia that never was. He hurls the artifact away, only for [[Branthan]]’s psyber‑eagle to snatch it.

The loyalists strike: Numen kills [[Marius Vairosean]], [[Grendel]] destroys Bombastus, and [[Nykona Sharrowkyn|Sharrowkyn]] slays [[Lucius]]. The gemstone is shattered, freeing [[Perturabo]] to strike [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]. The Primarch of the [[Emperor]]’s Children splits open and ascends as a Daemon Prince of Slaanesh, whisking his Legion away.

As Iydris collapses, [[Perturabo]] leads his sons to safety and orders the fleet into the black hole consuming the Crone World. [[Lucius]], resurrected by Slaanesh, awakens amid Fabius’s laboratory as the terata burn, leaving only one gene‑sample intact.

## 🔗 Connections
- Chronicles [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]]’s full transformation into a Daemon Prince.
- Deepens [[Perturabo]]’s character, his rivalry with [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Fulgrim|Fulgrim]], and his bitterness toward the Imperium.
- Features major Shattered Legion figures: [[Nykona Sharrowkyn|Sharrowkyn]], [[Sabik Wayland|Wayland]], [[Branthan]], Tarsa.
- Introduces the Eye of Terror’s naming.
- Advances the Iron Warriors’ internal politics: [[Kroeger]], [[Forrix]], Falk, [[Harkor]], [[Toramino]].
- Explores the [[Emperor]]’s Children’s descent into excess and mutation.
