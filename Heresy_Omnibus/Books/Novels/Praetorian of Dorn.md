# 🧭 Overview
[[Rogal Dorn]]'s Imperial Fists and Alpharius's Alpha Legion clash in a strategic duel fought across Sol‑bound intelligence networks, covert operations, and assassination attempts. As the traitors probe for weaknesses in Terra's defenses, the loyalists are forced to counter not just armies, but deception itself.

The novel is a key bridge toward the Siege of Terra, showing how the war increasingly turns on hidden moves as much as open battle.
