# 🧭 Overview
Deep beneath Terra, the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]] wages a secret war in the Webway while the Custodian Guard and Sisters of Silence fight to hold a collapsing front against daemonic invasion. At the same time, the wider Imperium fractures under the Heresy, placing impossible pressure on the Throneworld.

The novel is essential for understanding the [[Heresy_Omnibus/Characters/Emperor|Emperor]]'s long game, the burden of the Golden Throne, and why Terra becomes the final battleground of the conflict.
