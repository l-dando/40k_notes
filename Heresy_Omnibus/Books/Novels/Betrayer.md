# 🧭 Overview
*Betrayer* follows the World Eaters and Word Bearers as the Shadow Crusade burns through Ultramar, centering on the destructive bond between Angron and Lorgar. While cities and worlds fall, the story explores how faith, rage, and manipulation drive both Legions deeper into damnation.

It is one of the defining Heresy novels for understanding Angron's tragedy, Khârn's loyalty, and the scale of Chaos's influence on the war.
